Imagine the Future in 2050

Project 1




Description:

When told to envision the future in 2050, my mind immediately went to climate change. This is especially because of the discussions that we have today that about what we do today will effect the environment in the future. So to depict this, I wanted to showcase a little bit of all seasons, indicating that with climate change, the world’s environment will be out of control and these seasons will be ,merged into one. To show this, the left side of the image shows a drier desert climate and the right side of the road is a complete contrast with the snowy trees.

Another aspect that I wanted to tackle was the evolution of technology which is why the outer vignette shows binary numbers, which is the stereotypical depiction of deep programming. Also the elephant is actually an image of a holographic elephant that was taken a few years ago, but it also shows that in a way the natural world and the virtual world are going to collide in a way that we won’t be able to distinguish the two apart. I did this by manipulating the RGB channels in order to get a glitchy effect of the elephant.
